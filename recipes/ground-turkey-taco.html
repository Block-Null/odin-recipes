Ground Turkey Taco Meat

Description

A taco with spicy and different amazing flavors.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ tablespoons chili powder
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 12 ounces ground turkey
  • ½ cup water
  • 1 tablespoon cider vinegar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons brown sugar

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, garlic, onion powder, oregano, and cayenne in small bowl.
  3. Heat a large nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add ground turkey and cook stirring to break up clumps, until no longer pink, about 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in spice mixture and water. Reduce heat and simmer, stirring occasionally, until most of the liquid is absorbed, about 10 minutes.
  5. Stir in cider vinegar and brown sugar; simmer until flavors combine, 3 to 4 minutes.
  6. Transfer to a serving bowl.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
